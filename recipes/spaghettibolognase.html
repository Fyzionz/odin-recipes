Spaghetti Bolognase

Description

A warm wintery meal to feed your soul. Every family makes it differently! Best paired with a nice bottle of red.

Ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • cheese
  • beef
  • tomatoes

Steps

  1. Finely chop tomato
  2. Bring spaghetti to the boil
  3. Cook beef in a medium sized saucepan
  4. Add tomatoes
