Spaghetti Bolognase

Description

A nice healthy meal. Low on cost and better for the environment. Salads are a light meal best had for when you're feeling good and healthy.

Ingredients

  • cos lettuce
  • tomatoes
  • olives
  • almonds
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Finely chop tomato
  2. Add tomatoes, olives, almonds and cos lettuce to a bowl
  3. Drizzle olive oil
  4. Season with salt & pepper
